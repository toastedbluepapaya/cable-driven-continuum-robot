# cable-driven-continuum-robotic-manipulator

Undergraduate research project on the design, modeling, and development of a cable-driven continuum robotic manipulator with force feedback for minimally invasive surgery.

---

## Objectives
- Design a tendon-driven continuum manipulator
- Develop kinematic and mechanical models
- Integrate force sensing
- Evaluate dexterity and accuracy
- Explore teleoperation and haptic feedback
